Voting Information

If you are registered to vote in Ward 1, Sierra will be on your ballot. Visit the city website for more information about the election, including how to find your polling place. View a map of the wards above.

Register to Vote

The deadline for voter registration is 21 days prior to election day. Make sure you're registered to vote well in advance of the coming election. If you have changed your name, address, or party affiliation, you will need to re-register. Visit this website to check your registration and re-register if you need to.
